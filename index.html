Academic Research

Extent and glacial history of the Cordilleran Ice Sheet in northwest Montana: Using OSL to date glacial sediments from the southeastern Flathead Lobe

Abstract: This thesis investigates glacial sediments in the Flathead Valley, Polson, Montana that were deposited during the last glacial period (~115,000 – 11,700 years ago). Research objectives were to reconstruct the glacial history and timing of the furthest southeasterly extent of the Cordilleran Ice Sheet (CIS) at the Flathead Lobe in northwestern Montana and to assess the viability of luminescence dating of sediments in glacial environments..
